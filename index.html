What it does

One platform across the whole record.

From the moment a document arrives to the moment a clinician asks a question, IntelliCare turns scattered medical data into something your team can search, trust, and act on.

Conversational clinical AI

Ask in plain language. The assistant searches the full record, chains the right lookups across a patient's entire history, and answers with the sources it used.

Automated document analysis

Upload unstructured reports, labs, and discharge summaries. IntelliCare extracts thousands of structured fields automatically and files them where your team can find them.

Drug safety & decision support

Real-time interaction and contraindication checks against FDA data, with severity-rated alerts before a dangerous combination ever reaches the patient.

Documentation ready to share

Every record renders as a searchable, professionally formatted document with one-click PDF export — built for referrals, records requests, and care coordination.

Built for protected health information

Security is the foundation, not a feature.

IntelliCare is designed for healthcare compliance from the database up, so patient data stays isolated, encrypted, and accountable at every layer.

✓

HIPAA-aligned handling

PHI handling, breach notification, and configurable retention built in.

✓

Field-level encryption

AES-256-GCM at rest and TLS in transit, with a managed key hierarchy.

✓

Per-organization isolation

A separate database per organization — no shared patient data.

✓

Immutable audit logs

Every access to a record is recorded and cannot be altered.

✓

Role-based access control

Granular permissions and row-level filters on every query.

✓

PHI anonymization

De-identified data sets for research without exposing patients.
